About Us

We have been in the field of Beedi leaves and tobacco business for the past 25 years. As this is a small-scale cottage industry and our presence is in select parts of Tamil Nadu, the quality control is fully under our own supervision. The rural workers are skillful and efficient and have been in this line of work for decades. Hence the quality of GT Beedi is unparalleled till date and our brand name GT stands for “Good taste”. Our factory is situated in Tirunelveli, one of the oldest centres of Beedi industry. The rural workers know their trade and roll hundreds of Beedi everyday without a dent in the quality. Our motto is to manufacture and deliver the best products in terms of price and quality and to never compromise in taste and flavour.

Manufacturing Process

First Stage

The first stage includes sourcing the rich Gujarat and Nippani tobacco.

Second stage

The Beedi is rolled with Tendu leaves embedding this mixture in the middle and a small thread is tied at one end.

Third stage

The rolled Beedies are arranged in a huge tray and roasted in a wood husk/fire oven, this eliminates the moisture if any

Fourth stage

The roasted Beedies are then removed from the oven and then labelled and packed, with our trademark GT branding, which assures the genuineness of the product and the Beedis are ready to deliver.
